Your brand is more than

A brand is the sum of every experience someone has with your business — the words you use, the colours people see, the feeling they get. At Universal Agencies, we build brand systems that are cohesive, intentional, and built to last.

We work with retail shops, startups, and modern businesses to define who they are, what they stand for, and how they show up consistently across every touchpoint.

Our process starts with deep discovery — understanding your market, your competitors, and your customers — before a single pixel is placed. The result is a brand that is not just beautiful, but strategically sound.

Service 03

Influencer & Creator
Partnerships

We connect your brand with the right voices — authentic collaborations that build communities, trust, and real reach.

The right voice changes
everything.

In a world of endless content, people trust people — not ads. Influencer marketing done right is one of the highest-ROI channels available, but only when the match between creator and brand is authentic.

Universal Agencies manages the entire collaboration journey — from finding and vetting the right creators for your niche, to negotiating deals, managing deliverables, and measuring campaign performance.

We work with nano, micro, and macro influencers across fashion, food, lifestyle, tech, and more — ensuring your brand reaches the right audiences in the most credible way possible.

reach.

What is included

Creator Discovery and Vetting

Campaign Brief and Strategy

Contract and Deal Management

We handle negotiations, contracts, and payments so you do not have to deal with the back-and-forth.

Content Execution

Performance Analytics

Long-term Creator Relations
